Privacy Policy for BillEase

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how BillEase collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

BillEase does not collect or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter into the app — such as your bills, calendar entries, and savings information — is stored locally on your device.

2. AI Import Feature

The “Import from Notes” feature processes your text using on-device or cloud-based AI services depending on platform capabilities. BillEase does not store or share your imported text. It is used only to help create bill entries inside the app.

3. No Account Required

BillEase does not require you to sign up or create an account. We do not collect email addresses, passwords, or personal identities.

4. Third-Party Services

BillEase may use third-party tools such as analytics or crash reporting services in the future. If used, they will comply with Apple’s privacy guidelines.

5. Children

BillEase is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
