Privacy Policy for BillEase

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how BillEase collects, stores, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

BillEase uses Firebase Authentication to create and manage user accounts. When you sign up or log in, Firebase may collect your email address or other login credentials depending on the authentication method you choose.

BillEase stores your bills, due dates, and related data in a secure Firebase database. This allows you to access your information across devices and ensures it is backed up safely in the cloud.

2. AI Import Feature

The “Import from Notes” feature processes your text using on-device or cloud-based AI services depending on platform capabilities. BillEase does not permanently store or share your imported text outside of adding the bill entries to your account.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used only to provide the core functions of the app, including:

  • Storing bill information and due dates
  • Syncing your data securely across devices
  • Managing your account

4. Third-Party Services

BillEase uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – for account creation and login
  • Firebase Database (Firestore or Realtime Database) – for securely storing your bill entries

These services follow Google’s privacy and security standards.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

6. Children

BillEase is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children without parental consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated date.
