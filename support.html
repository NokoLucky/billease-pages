Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a bill?

Go to the Bills page, tap “Add Bill,” then enter the name, amount, and due date.

2. How does data storage work?

BillEase uses Firebase to store your bills and account information securely in the cloud. This means your data is safe and accessible across devices when you log in.

3. What does Firebase Authentication do?

Firebase Authentication allows you to sign up or log in using your email or other supported login methods. This ensures your bill data is linked to your account and stored securely.

4. How do I use the AI Import feature?

Tap “Import from Notes,” paste your text or upload a note, and BillEase will automatically detect and create bill entries for you.

5. How do I view my bills on the calendar?

Go to the Calendar page to see your bills organized by date.
