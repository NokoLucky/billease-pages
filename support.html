Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a bill?

Go to the Bills page, tap “Add Bill,” then enter the name, amount, and due date.

2. How do I use the AI Import feature?

Tap “Import from Notes,” paste your text or upload a note, and BillEase will automatically detect and add your bills.

3. Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. BillEase does not upload your information to external servers.

4. How do I view my bills on the calendar?

Go to the Calendar page to see all your bills organized by date.
